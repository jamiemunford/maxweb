Max Boehme

Bio

Born in Hart in 1966, lives and works in Vienna, Waldhausen in the Waldviertel and New Plymouth, New Zealand. 1986-1992 studied at the University of Applied Arts in Vienna, with Ernst Caramelle, Peter Weibel and Ferdinand Schmatz. 1998-1999 Lecturer at the Aktsaal at the University of Applied Arts in Vienna since 2001 Sensationsverlag: Artist books in fine edition since 2006 Kunstkumpel Waldhausen, Sommerfest am Mayerhof. since 2007 smoking room: Offspace with exhibitions, book presentations, readings and concerts, from September 2012 in guest in the Secession, Vienna
